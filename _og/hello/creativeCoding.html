parsons data visualization coursework

As part of the Parson's Data Visualization MS program, I am currently learning a wide variety of communication and creation tools including p5.js and various machine learning models. The final projects shown are The Doomsday Clock, an Earthquake Critical Facilities , and the Explorative Exports on Nuclear Weapons Testing. You can also find an experimental machine learning project working on clustering art collections based on metadata here.
