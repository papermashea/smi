department of labor

usability research and prototyping in civic tech

My work with the Department of Labor as a consultant and prototyper for a large-scale website redesign led by rayogram, a creative agency based in TriBeCa.
My contribution to this project has so far consisted of an in-depth usability study examining the audience of the DoL website based current website analytics, task completion metrics, and user surveys. I was also brought in to help build out the tablet and mobile prototypes using Adobe XD as a collaboration with the design team at rayogram.
