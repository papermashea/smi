eddi

electrodialysis desalinator for irrigation

EDDI was an award-winning electrodialysis desalinator for irrigation designed to provide real-time soil and salinity metrics to small-scale farmers in California trying to avoid overwatering and leeching.

Using the mobile device, a user could remotely turn off their personal desalinator and desalinate only to the necessary levels to save water and energy. As the design lead on the team, I created the visual identity, built the mobile and web interfaces and produced all the multimedia assets as well as helped build the machine. This project went on to live on a permaculture barge in the East River.
