little cinema

"rocky horror picture show meets cirque du soleil"

Little Cinema is an immersive film remix project based out of House of Yes in Brooklyn. As a production assistant on the Little Cinema team, I've made props, costumes, participatory activations and digital promotional materials. In my time with Little Cinema, we have developed experimental experiences with The Brooklyn Museum, Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival, and the Future of Story Telling Festival

Besides Little Cinema's exciting growth and creative approach, the team behind Little Cinema has been a huge inspiration to me personally and professionally.
