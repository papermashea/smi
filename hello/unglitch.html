unglitch

art-technology activism

Unglitch is an art-tech activist organization that is currently focusing on net neutrality and tech policy education and outreach initiatives in New York City. As one of the founders and primary organizer, I helped develop the objectives and visual identity to secure locations and sponsorships for a alternative networks panel, an all-day hackathon at fat cat fab labs and an afterparty at babycastles.

We have since recieved a neighborhood grant from the Citizen's Committee for New York City, booked space for Northside Festival Art Walk in June and are planning an installation for Pioneer Work's Software for Artists' Day in July.
